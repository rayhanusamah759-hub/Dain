# DAIN Vision

## What is DAIN?

DAIN (Digital Archive & Intelligent Notebook) is an AI-native Research Operating System.

It is designed to become the central knowledge base for research, simulations, publications, collaborations, and AI-assisted work.

---

## Goals

- Preserve research knowledge
- Organize projects
- Track experiments
- Record important decisions
- Help AI understand research context
- Automate repetitive work

---

## Core Principles

1. Knowledge over files
2. AI-readable by design
3. Every important decision is recorded
4. Everything is connected
5. Open and portable
